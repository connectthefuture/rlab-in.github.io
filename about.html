More About Our Organisation.

We are a small non profitable organisation with full of robotics enthusiast and dedicated to make robotics fun. Our main goal is to make robotics technology easily acccessible so that more young minds aspire towards this field. We are developing our competence in the area of Humanoid robotics movement and control, Compliant soft robotics for safe human-robot interaction and mobile robotics and navigation. We take up research projects from academia and industries and provide a platform and an online environment for any young engineer or amateur to participate, learn and contribute in the project.
