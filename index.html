Vision

If you are interested in robotics or humanoid robotics in particular and would like to pursue your research interest in this field in future but demotivated by the fact that access to the robots is expensive and limited to few world class laboratories and univesities, then this is the place for you. Our effort is to provide a platform where you can learn about advances in robotic technology, be the part of research projects offered and supervised by the researchers around the world and even able to perform experiments on the robots to suport your research.

Cources

Fundamental knowledge of mathematics, kinematics, dynamics and control is neccessary to conduct research in robotics. Also the computer programming skills, basic electronics and embedded system are nontheless important. Here not only we provide vast resources for self learning but also offer short courses, some of them are supervised, which will provide you indepth understanding of such concepts.
